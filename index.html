About Us

Kinga Housing Co-operative Society Ltd was established and registered in March, 2012. The purpose of establishing it was to grow members’ wealth through prudent investment ventures and provide members with housing accommodation at fair and reasonable prices.

Objective

Short term: Speculate in the real estate market by purchasing land at competitive rates, subdividing it and re-selling it in order to realize a profit.

Long-term: Secure a roof over members’ heads, by providing members with living accommodation at a fair and reasonable price.

PROJECTS

Project I: KITENGELA
Fully Sold Out
The Society acquired 10 acres in Kitengela, Kajiado County in year 2014. Sub-divided it into 64, 1/8 acre plots. Project is fully sold out and Title deeds issued to members.
